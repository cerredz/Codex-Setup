## Scoring Framework (Practical Use)
- Reliability score (1-5): How often recommendations align with verifiable systems behavior.
- Reproducibility score (1-5): How easily claims can be tested with normal analytics/search tooling.
- Business proximity score (1-5): How directly advice maps to revenue, leads, or cost efficiency.
- Risk score (1-5): Probability of policy/legal/reputation downside if applied naively.
